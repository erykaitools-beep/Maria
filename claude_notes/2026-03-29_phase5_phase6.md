### Phase 5: Effector Safety Envelope (ADR-026)

5-level staged authority for OpenClaw effector:

| Level | Behavior | Dangerous | Safe |
|-------|----------|-----------|------|
| OBSERVE (default) | BLOCK | block | block |
| SUGGEST | Notify operator, no exec | notify | notify |
| CONFIRM | Queue for approval, exec after confirm | approve | approve |
| BOUNDED | Auto-exec safe, confirm dangerous | approve | auto |
| UNRESTRICTED | Stub (blocked in Phase 5) | - | - |
