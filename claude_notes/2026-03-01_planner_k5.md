## Kluczowe decyzje

1. **ADR-013: Rule-based v1** - zero LLM w petli decyzyjnej. Deterministic, testable.
2. **Phase 10 replacement** - PlannerCore zastepuje teacher auto-trigger z backward-compatible fallback.
3. **Hybrid frequency** - co 60 tickow + event-driven na exam_result, alert, user_command, sandbox_promoted.
